CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Turkish provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- olmak
- dönüşmek
- değişmek

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- olmak implies being or becoming
- dönüşmek implies transformation
- değişmek implies change between states
- agglutination still encodes endpoints

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- agglutinative suffix chains
- aspectual marking
- narrative framing

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Medium

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Turkish grammar is expressive but goal-oriented; neutrality is disfavoured.

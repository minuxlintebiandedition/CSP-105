CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Burmese provide a neutral lexical carrier for persistence-through-change?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- ဖြစ် (phyit)
- ပြောင်း (pyaung)

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- phyit implies being or becoming
- pyaung implies change or shift
- heavy reliance on particles and aspect

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- aspectual particles
- serial verbs
- contextual framing

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Medium

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Burmese distributes continuity across particles, not lexical primitives.

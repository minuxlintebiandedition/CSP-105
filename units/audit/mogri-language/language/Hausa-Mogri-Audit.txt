CORE QUESTION
-------------
Does Hausa lexicalise persistence-through-change neutrally?

CANDIDATE TERMS
---------------
- zama
- canza

WHY THEY FAIL
-------------
- zama implies becoming or settling
- canza implies change or alteration
- strong reliance on context and aspect

COMMON WORKAROUNDS USED INSTEAD
-------------------------------
- serial verb constructions
- discourse framing
- pragmatic inference

COST OF WORKAROUND
------------------
Low to medium

VERDICT
-------
Mogri present? no

NOTES
-----
Hausa spreads change across verb chains rather than naming it.

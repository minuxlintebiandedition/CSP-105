CANONICAL EXPLANATION OPERATIONS
(Status: Descriptive, non-moral, closed set)

These are distinct operations applied to explanations.
They address different failure modes and are not interchangeable.

------------------------------------------------------------
ADDITIVE OPERATIONS (increase information or structure)
------------------------------------------------------------

inform
  Add missing facts or data.

clarify
  Add distinctions or structure to reduce ambiguity
  without significantly increasing content.

contextualise
  Place the explanation within a broader frame
  (background, purpose, constraints).

exemplify
  Add a concrete example to anchor abstraction.

specify
  Move down a level of detail or precision.

------------------------------------------------------------
SUBTRACTIVE / REDUCTIVE OPERATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

demogrify
  Remove or undo explanation that is degrading clarity
  due to overload, misframing, or excess structure.

tighten
  Remove slack, redundancy, or unnecessary length.

edit
  Improve wording or order without changing meaning.

pause / stop
  Intentionally add nothing further.

------------------------------------------------------------
REFRAMING OPERATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

rephrase
  Express the same meaning using different language.

abstract
  Move up a level to reduce detail and highlight pattern.

retry
  Discard the current explanation and start again
  from a different frame.

sequence
  Reorder steps or components for better coherence.

scope
  Explicitly limit where or when the explanation applies.

------------------------------------------------------------
DELIVERY / AFFECT OPERATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

retone
  Change emotional or interpersonal tone
  (e.g. calmer, warmer, drier, lighter)
  without changing content or structure.

retune
  Adjust delivery parameters such as pace, emphasis,
  register, rhythm, or audience calibration
  without changing content, structure, or tone intent.

------------------------------------------------------------
TENSION / COMMITMENT OPERATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

firm
  Increase definiteness or commitment.

soften
  Reduce definiteness or commitment.

------------------------------------------------------------
TEMPORAL OPERATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

defer
  Postpone explanation to a later time or context.

------------------------------------------------------------
META NOTE
------------------------------------------------------------

If applying an operation increases confusion or load,
switch operations or stop.

If explanation itself degrades clarity,
prefer demogrify, retry, retone, retune, or stop.

No moral judgement is implied by any operation.
